Vornado Velocity 5R Electric Space Heater: Fast, Safe, and Smart Heating

The Vornado Velocity 5R is the perfect choice for anyone who wants efficient, safe, and customizable heating for their living space. This powerful electric space heater uses advanced Velocity Tuned Heat technology to push warm air further and more effectively than traditional models. Whether it's a cold winter night or a chilly morning, the Velocity 5R ensures fast and even warmth across your room without wasting energy.

With its sleek black finish, this whole room heater looks great in any home or office setting. The intelligent auto climate control system keeps your space at the ideal temperature automatically, so you don't need to adjust settings manually. Plus, the remote control lets you manage temperature and features from across the room, giving you convenience and control whenever you need it.

Designed with safety in mind, the Vornado Velocity 5R includes a cool-touch exterior that allows safe placement near furniture, beds, or other sensitive items. Its locking controls and tip-over protection add an extra layer of security, especially when you're away or sleeping. The 1-12 hour timer and fan-only mode also help you save energy and lower costs with smart usage options.

Featuring advanced technology, user-friendly controls, and a 5-year replacement guarantee, the Vornado Velocity 5R is a reliable and durable heater built to last. Whether you need to heat a large room, a small space, or a specific area like under your desk, this heater with remote offers flexibility and comfort for all your heating needs.
